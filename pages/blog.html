Advice to Newbies in Programming

Concept of Programming to newbies can be so tiresome that they want to give up in no sooner time. Beginners are advised to do repeative learning so as to master the basics of programming. It won't take long, programming concepts will become second-nature to daring newbies. In terms of web development, first comers should concentrate on html, css and javascript, learning the ins and outs before proceding to frameworks. Frameworks ride on the back of fundamentals; newbies that are not so grounded get lost when opinionated concepts of frameworks are introduced.
